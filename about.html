Our Story

From raw images → structured intelligence → decisions you can trust.

Built for real-world deployment: fast, secure, cloud-ready.

VisionForge AI is focused on making visual data genuinely useful. A photo by itself is unstructured. We turn it into structured signals: objects, scenes, text, context, and summaries — then we store and organize those outputs so you can search, filter, and learn from your images over time.

Our systems are designed for the modern web: scalable services, clean interfaces, and API-first design. We build with practical engineering constraints in mind — latency, cost, security, and maintainability — so the work doesn’t stop at a demo.

This course project evolves from a polished static company site (Project 1) into a full SPA (Project 2) where users connect a photo source (e.g., Pinterest or Google Photos), analyze images using Google Vision + Gemini, and store results in a cloud database for later exploration.

What We Build

Non-trivial AI features that are product-like, not just “image processing”.

Vision Intelligence

Detect objects, scenes, labels, text, and metadata to structure photo content.

Summaries & Insights

Use foundation models to explain images, cluster themes, and generate useful summaries.

Cloud Storage & Retrieval

Persist results in a database so users can explore history and compare over time.
